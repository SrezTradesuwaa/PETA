About Pemuteran Tourism

Pemuteran Village in Bali is well known for its marine conservation, beautiful coral reefs, and sustainable tourism development. This project introduces a digital map showing ecotourism and agrotourism locations that visitors can explore.

Tourism Activities

Ecotourism

Explore coral reef conservation and marine biodiversity.

Agrotourism

Discover local farms, grapes, and traditional agriculture.

Local Culture

Experience Balinese culture and village traditions.
